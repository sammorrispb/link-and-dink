# Week 8 — Design a Clinic

*Week eight is a design week. Before you run a clinic in week nine, you build it — as a curriculum, not an improvisation. This is the session-design muscle's biggest test.*

---

## The theme

A clinic is a small-group lesson scaled up and themed — and the word that matters is *design*. An improvised clinic is a basket of balls with a flyer. A designed clinic is a curriculum compressed into ninety minutes. Week eight is entirely about the design: building something that will keep the promise its theme makes.

## Your reps

- **Choose a theme and design around it.** Pick a clinic theme — usually one block of [the Adult Skill Progression](../frameworks/the-adult-skill-progression). Then [design the clinic](../systems/design-a-clinic): a climbing sequence of blocks or stations, every minute of it serving the theme.
- **Run the full Session Design Framework, scaled up.** [The Session Design Framework](../frameworks/session-design-framework) — one focus, know your players, choose games that rep it, sequence easy to hard, script the frame and the off-ramps. At clinic scale, with stations and a bigger room.
- **Design for the level spread from the start.** A clinic sign-up sheet doesn't filter for level. Build [the Mixed-Level Playbook](../frameworks/the-mixed-level-playbook) into the design — don't bolt it on later.
- **Review it with Sam.** Walk the whole clinic design through [the Lesson-Plan Review Flow](../flows/lesson-plan-review-flow) — the six questions, at clinic scale.

## The 1:1

Week eight's 1:1 is a working session: Sam **co-designs** the clinic with you. Not "approves it" — builds it alongside you, asking the review questions in real time, helping you find the weak block on paper. You leave the 1:1 with a clinic design that's ready to run in week nine.

## What it teaches

- [How to Design a Clinic](../systems/design-a-clinic) — the curriculum-not-a-class discipline.
- [The Session Design Framework](../frameworks/session-design-framework) — the design process at its largest scale.
- [The Adult Skill Progression](../frameworks/the-adult-skill-progression) — where the clinic theme comes from.
- [The Lesson-Plan Review Flow](../flows/lesson-plan-review-flow) — pressure-testing the design before week nine.

## The standard

By the end of week eight you have a fully designed, Sam-co-designed clinic: one clear theme, a climbing sequence, every block with a stated purpose, the level spread built in, the frame scripted, every off-ramp decided. You haven't run it yet — that's week nine. The standard for week eight is a *design that's ready*, on paper, that survived the six questions.
