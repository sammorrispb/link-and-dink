# Week 2 — The Pillars, and Your First Reps

*"Real reps from week one" — and here they are. Week two is when you stop watching and start doing. Co-running, not solo yet, but your hands are on the work.*

---

## The theme

Mentorship without reps is a workshop. Week two is the apprenticeship proving it isn't one. You learn the two Pillars — the standard you'll audit every session against for the rest of your career — and then you go put them on a court, with Sam right next to you.

## Your reps

- **Learn the Pillars cold.** [The Coach Up Pillars](../frameworks/the-coach-up-pillars) — Active Court and Feedback Density. Not memorized as a slogan; understood as the two-question audit you'll run on every session forever.
- **Co-run a pickup.** Alongside Sam. You take real responsibility — a rotation, a stretch of the room, the energy — while Sam runs the rest and watches you. This is the bridge between shadowing and solo.
- **Your first paid reps.** This is where the "earn while you learn" promise becomes literal. The reps are real, the players are real, the stakes are gentle but not zero.
- **Your first self-assessment and debrief.** Every session you touch this week gets the [seven-box self-assessment](../frameworks/the-apprentice-self-assessment) and a [post-event debrief](../flows/post-event-debrief-flow). The habit starts now and never stops.

## The 1:1

Week two's 1:1 debriefs the co-run honestly — what you saw yourself do, where the Pillars held and where they slipped, what Sam observed. It also looks ahead: Sam starts prepping you for [the first solo event](../flows/first-event-flow), which lands in week four. Your "one thing to watch for" from week one comes back into the conversation here.

## What it teaches

- [The Coach Up Pillars](../frameworks/the-coach-up-pillars) — the two non-negotiables of every session.
- [How to Run a Pickup](../systems/run-a-pickup) — the apprentice's bread-and-butter event format.
- [The Apprentice Self-Assessment](../frameworks/the-apprentice-self-assessment) — the seven-box check, starting this week.
- [The Post-Event Debrief Flow](../flows/post-event-debrief-flow) — the keystone habit, also starting this week.

## The standard

By the end of week two you've run real reps, you can name the two Pillars and what each demands, and you've completed your first honest self-assessment and debrief. It won't be smooth — co-running is meant to be a little awkward. The standard isn't smooth; it's *real reps, honestly debriefed.*

> Week two: learn the two Pillars, co-run with Sam, take your first paid reps — and start the self-assessment and debrief habits that run for the next ten weeks and beyond.
