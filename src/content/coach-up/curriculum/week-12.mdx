# Week 12 — Graduation

*Twelve weeks. The apprenticeship doesn't end with a certificate in the mail — it ends with a clear, honest answer to one question: are you ready to coach on your own?*

---

## The theme

Week twelve is not a victory lap and it's not an exam. It's a clean, honest look at the coach you've become — through a capstone event, an honest self-score, and a three-way sign-off. The full mechanics are in [The Graduation Flow](../flows/graduation-flow); this week is where they run.

## Your reps

- **Run the final observed solo event.** A capstone — a full event, solo, Sam observing under the silent-observer rule. The same setup as [your first event in week four](week-04), now as a measure of twelve weeks of growth.
- **Self-score the full rubric.** Score yourself, honestly, against [The Coach Competency Rubric](../progression/the-coach-competency-rubric) — the [self-assessment](../frameworks/the-apprentice-self-assessment) habit scaled up to the whole apprenticeship. Where are you strong, where are you still thin, what's your real read on your own readiness.
- **Complete the three-yes sign-off.** Graduation requires three aligned yeses: **yours** (the honest self-score), **Sam's** ("I'd send my own players to this coach"), and **the pattern's** (a consistent record across the cohort — see [The Readiness Bar](../progression/the-readiness-bar)).

## The 1:1

Week twelve's 1:1 is the graduation decision itself. Because [week eleven's readiness check-in](week-11) already surfaced anything outstanding, this conversation holds no surprises — it makes the call official.

- **Three yeses → you graduate to Coach.** You move onto [The Coach Up Ladder](../progression/the-coach-up-ladder) at the Coach tier, running your own practice with the apprenticeship's scaffolding behind you and the mentorship continuing in a lighter form.
- **Short of three → the apprenticeship extends**, with one named focus area and a defined runway. This is the system's value, held to the end: **we don't fail for slow.** An extension is support and a clear target, not a door.

- [The Coach Up Ladder](../progression/the-coach-up-ladder) — what graduation moves you onto.

## The standard

The standard for week twelve is not perfection — it's *readiness*, honestly assessed and honestly agreed. A capstone event that's real, a self-score that's brave, and three yeses that line up. And if they don't line up yet, an extension that names exactly what to work on. Either way, week twelve ends with the truth — which is the only thing the apprenticeship was ever going to graduate you with.

> Week twelve: a capstone event, an honest full-rubric self-score, a three-yes sign-off. Three yeses graduate you to Coach. Short of that, you extend with a named focus — because we don't fail for slow. Twelve weeks, and an honest answer.
